About

“Life is like a card game; You can bluff, fold, or win big, it’s all how you play the game”

Playing cards have been the world's favourite pass time for centuries now. In India, the game became popular at the Mughal courts and soon caught the attention of the general public. We surely can’t get the days of Mughal courts back but we’ve built an exclusive online court for you.

We’ve got you some classic card games that most Indians play at home and created the same experience but with a bigger home: The internet. Our beta version offers Teen-Do-Paanch, a game commonly played in the Indian subcontinent. You can either chose to play against our pool of community users or try our bot. You’ve sent us suggestions to include games like Satti Center, Dehla Pakad etc. As you start indulging in our application, we shall soon seek your vote on the launch of our next game. You decide what you want to play next and we get our hands on multiple lines of code to present it to you.

Passtime it may be but unflinching concentration and involvement could lead to violent disagreements in what some call the Devil’s game. We’ve made sure you are physically apart but try sparing the screen.

For any queries or feedback, we would love to hear from you
